What We Do

Residential Design

We work with homeowners to create beautiful and comfortable living spaces.At SVS Living Interiors, we believe your home should be a reflection of your unique story. We specialize in transforming houses into beautiful and comfortable living spaces that are both stylish and highly functional. Our expert team works closely with you to understand your needs and bring your vision to life, ensuring every detail, from the layout to the decor, is tailored to you. We are committed to crafting interiors that are not only visually stunning but also enhance your lifestyle.

Commercial Design

We help businesses craft productive and visually appealing workspaces, including offices, retail stores, and cafes. We focus on layouts that improve workflow and reflect your brand.We specialize in creating bespoke commercial spaces that not only look incredible but also enhance productivity and brand identity. From dynamic corporate offices and welcoming retail stores to cozy cafes, we collaborate with you to design a layout that optimizes workflow and reflects your company's core values. Our approach focuses on using innovative materials and smart design to build an environment where your business can thrive, leaving a lasting impression on clients and employees alike.

Renovations design

Our renovation services are all about breathing new life into your existing space. Whether you're looking to update a single room or completely transform your entire home, we combine modern design principles with your home’s unique charm. We carefully manage every detail, from initial planning to final touches, ensuring a seamless process that revitalizes your space and makes it more functional and beautiful than ever before. Our goal is to create a fresh look while preserving the character and history you love.

Vintage Projects

We specialize in bringing the elegance of vintage design to life. Our projects focus on preserving classic elements while integrating modern comfort and functionality for a truly timeless space. With our vintage projects, we celebrate the elegance of the past while seamlessly integrating the comforts of the present. We specialize in sourcing authentic period pieces and materials to create a truly unique atmosphere. Our team meticulously works to restore classic elements like intricate woodwork or antique fixtures, then thoughtfully pairs them with modern amenities for a space that is both functional and timeless. This service is for clients who appreciate classic design and want their home to tell a rich, historical story.
